Available Financial Aid Programs

🏛️ New York State Programs

  • TAP - Tuition Assistance Program (up to $5,165)
  • Excelsior Scholarship - Free SUNY/CUNY tuition (up to $5,500)
  • Enhanced Tuition Award - Private college aid (up to $6,000)
  • NYS Dream Act - Aid for undocumented students (up to $5,000)

🇺🇸 Federal Programs

  • Pell Grant - Federal need-based grant (up to $6,895)
  • SEOG - Supplemental Educational Opportunity Grant (up to $4,000)
